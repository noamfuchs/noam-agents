- **2018-2020:** Zikhron Yaakov youth work: volunteer director leading 3 teams of 30 volunteers, youth festivals ("Indie Youth", "Night at the Museum"). National service at Kfar HaYarkon (Ramat Hasharon) as music teacher and sound instructor. (CVs, recommendation letters)
- **Age 18 (~2018):** decided to switch from sound/drums to keyboards and production. Trained 6-8 hours a day for ~4 years, nights and early mornings, while serving and working. (Reichman application)

- **2021-2024:** worked with top Israeli artists: Static (keyboardist + band manager, ~15 months of weekly shows for tens of thousands), Ivri Lider, Ninet Tayeb, Nadav Dagon, Daniel Solomon, Omer Yefet, Noa Kirel and others (see [[music]] for the full archive list). Learned French (A2), attended finance and M&A lectures, mentored by Constantine (Chicago MBA, finance executive). Insight there: education is "the only thing worth going into debt for". (HEC Lausanne letter)
- **May 2024:** completed the full bagrut in 5 months, 7 subjects, GPA 96 (5-unit physics and English, 4-unit math). Normally a ~1.5 year track. (matriculation coordinator's letter)
- **2024-2025:** accepted to Ben Gurion, Technion, and Reichman; applied to HEC Lausanne and St. Gallen; chose Reichman (Adelson School of Entrepreneurship, economics + entrepreneurship + data science). (acceptance letters, applications)
- **May-Nov 2025:** OD SIFRA, investor relations in US residential real estate: deal sourcing, due diligence, LLC formation. The bridge into the real-estate world that became [[real-estate-course]] and [[crm-rea]]. (CV) ^[inferred: the causal link]
