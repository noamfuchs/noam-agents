## Topics

### Platform Scope & Rationale

  - [The platform's scope was expanded beyond a simple CEO pipeline to increase user adoption.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=131.0)
  - [**Rationale:** A standalone pipeline offers little value to community members, who would only use it out of obligation.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=229.0)
  - [**New Core Features:**](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=152.0)
      - [**CEO Search:** A public directory for members to browse and connect with CEOs.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=152.0)
      - [**Meeting Calendar:** A central hub for group and community-wide meeting schedules.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=641.0)
      - [**CEO Pipeline:** The original internal tool for managers to track outreach.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=540.0)
  - [**Out of Scope (for now):** The application/onboarding pipeline.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=725.0)
      - [**Rationale:** To maintain focus and meet deadlines. It remains a future goal.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=769.0)

### User Experience (UX) & Feature Definition

  - [**Two-Tiered Access:**](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1800.0)
      - [**Community Members:** A clean, public-facing directory of CEO profiles.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1800.0)
      - [**Managers/Owners:** Full access to the detailed internal pipeline for operational tracking.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1800.0)
  - [**CEO Profile Enhancements:**](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=960.0)
      - [**Self-Editable Fields:** CEOs can update their own profiles, including what they are looking for (e.g., "Data Analyst").](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=960.0)
      - [**Search by Need:** This data will enable members to search for CEOs based on specific needs, facilitating more relevant connections.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=960.0)
      - [**Contact Info Privacy:** CEOs can choose to make their contact info public or require members to go through their assigned contact person.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1374.0)
  - [**CEO Status Visibility:**](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1408.0)
      - [**Problem:** The raw internal pipeline view is unprofessional and confusing for members.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1408.0)
      - [**Solution:** A simplified status system for the public view.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1667.0)
          - [**"Coming Soon" Category:** Highlights CEOs scheduled for upcoming meetings.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1911.0)
          - [**Binary Status on Search:** A member searching for a CEO will see if they are already in the pipeline, preventing duplicate outreach without revealing internal details like "Not Relevant."](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1715.0)
  - [**Meeting Management:**](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1800.0)
      - [**Member Role:** Propose CEOs for meetings.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1800.0)
      - [**Manager Role:** Approve and schedule proposed CEOs.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=1800.0)
  - [**Group Management:**](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=2016.0)
      - [**Manager View:** A dedicated dashboard for each manager's specific group, showing members, meeting history, and future plans.](https://fathom.video/calls/701765815?tab=summary&timestamp=2016.0)
