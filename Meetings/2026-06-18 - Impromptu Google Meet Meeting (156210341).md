### The Problem: Inefficient Medical Data Acquisition

  - [Dana's experience highlights the core issue: acquiring medical data is a major bottleneck.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1263.0)
  - [**Key Challenges:**](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1263.0)
      - [**Bureaucracy:** Slow, complex vendor negotiations.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1020.0)
      - [**Quality Mismatches:** Late discovery of unusable data (e.g., slides scanned at 20x magnification when 40x is required).](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1263.0)
      - [**Lack of Standardization:** No common format or quality metrics across vendors.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1263.0)
      - [**Regulatory Hurdles:** The difficulty of anonymizing sensitive patient data for compliance.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1200.0)

### The Solution: A Niche Medical Data Marketplace

  - [The platform would act as a "data bank," connecting data holders (hospitals) with data users (MedTech, researchers).](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1188.0)
  - [**Core Strategy:**](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1435.0)
      - [**Niche Focus:** Start with a single vertical (e.g., pathology slides) to build deep expertise and solve specific pain points before expanding.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1435.0)
      - [**Standardization:** Enforce data quality and format standards to eliminate guesswork.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1263.0)
      - [**Compliance:** Streamline the anonymization process to reduce the regulatory burden on data providers.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1200.0)
  - [**Value Proposition:**](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1589.0)
      - [**For Hospitals:** Create a new revenue stream from unused data archives.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1589.0)
      - [**For Users:** Provide a reliable, standardized source of data, accelerating research and product development.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1263.0)
      - [**For the Industry:** Enable better AI models and academic research by providing access to fresh, diverse datasets.](https://fathom.video/share/LDvSGqPTgtSYaXrrSN_wU1cunQxmvYPg?tab=summary&timestamp=1632.0)
