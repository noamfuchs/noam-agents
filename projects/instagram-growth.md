# Instagram Growth

**Goal:** Grow Noam's personal Instagram from **1,030 followers (2026-06-07)** to **5,000 by 2027-06**. Optimize purely for reach now, monetize later.
**Language:** Hebrew first, English versions added from ~month 9.
**Format:** Mix of talking-head and voiceover/b-roll, lean into whatever feels natural per topic.
**Cadence:** 2 reels/week. Tuesday + Motzei Shabbat. Sunday is editing day.
**Origin:** Strategy session with IG specialist (Hadekel), 2026-06-07. See [[rfs-global]] for the RFS pillar source.

---

## The spine (positioning)

A multi-topic personal brand fails when a stranger can't tell what they're following. The fix is one identity that makes every topic make sense together:

> **An Israeli founder going all-in on building an ambitious life across every dimension: company, body, mind, and a cross-cultural love story. Out loud.**

The unfair advantage is the **relationship pillar** (Noam + a Chinese partner). Cross-cultural-love content is one of the highest-reach Reel formats and almost no Israeli founder/fitness creator has it. It is the top-of-funnel magnet that pulls people into the rest.

**Bio direction (draft):** one line that says who he is in 3 seconds, e.g. *"בונה חברה, גוף, וזוגיות בין-תרבותית. מתעד הכל."* Refine once pillars are validated.

## The two tests (every reel passes both before posting)

1. **Hook test** — do the first 1.5 seconds make someone stop? Write the hook *before* filming.
2. **Follow test** — after watching, is it obvious what they get by following?

Two reels/week that pass both beats five that don't.
