## Topics

### The Problem: Consumer Friction & Lost Money

  - [Consumers lose money on unwanted subscriptions and unclaimed insurance refunds due to high friction (e.g., complex forms, long phone calls).](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=245.0)
  - [**Personal Examples:**](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=245.0)
      - [Dana discovered a forgotten $10/month subscription via email scan.](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=230.0)
      - [She is personally eligible for ₪4,000 in unclaimed refunds (₪2k for private services, ₪2k for psychology), which she hasn't pursued due to the hassle.](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=743.0)
  - [**The Opportunity:** An investor's partner (from the company Fondo) built a business *adding* friction to prevent cashback claims. Dana's idea is to do the opposite—*remove* friction for the consumer.](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=420.0)

### The Solution: Biliyo AI Agent

  - [Biliyo is an AI agent that connects to a user's email to:](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=245.0)
    1.  [**Subscription Management:** Identify and cancel/downgrade unwanted subscriptions.](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=245.0)
    2.  [**Refund Filing:** Analyze expenses and file insurance claims on the user's behalf.](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=245.0)
  - [**MVP Scope:** The initial MVP will focus on scanning emails to identify refund opportunities.](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=555.0)
  - [**Future Vision:** Expand to proactive services, like setting automatic cancellation reminders for student discounts when a degree ends.](https://fathom.video/share/oxQdgY1yNgnnMMRWj7ty5cFT8KxRoaX5?tab=summary&timestamp=660.0)
