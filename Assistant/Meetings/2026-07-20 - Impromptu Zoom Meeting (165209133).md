### Problem: Role & Communication Misalignment

### Yonatan's Vision: Top-Down Expansion

  - [**Strategic Shift:** The "Global" initiative is not a new niche but the organization's next major step, requiring a unified, company-wide effort.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=1000.0)
  - [**Accelerated Launch:** Leverage existing assets to launch faster and more effectively than the initial Israel effort.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=2154.0)
      - [**Assets:** Existing network of CEOs, English-language videos, and a proven model.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=2173.0)
      - [**Infrastructure:** An immediate path to establish a US NGO via an existing partner (Marei Pered), providing a US bank account and legal/tax framework.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=2249.0)
  - [**Funding & Compensation:** The organization's strong unit economics and brand value create a path to raise $50k+ from passionate investors. This funding could be used to pay key US community managers, which is seen as necessary for success given the team's remote location.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=2183.0)

### Noam's Vision: Bottom-Up Expansion

  - [**People-First Approach:** The organization's success is driven by its people and energy, not a formal business model.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=1330.0)
  - [**Organic Growth:** Start with one NYC group and focus on embedding the organization's DNA through close mentorship.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=1706.0)
      - [**Rationale:** This ensures quality and preserves the organization's core values, which are critical for a volunteer-based model.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=1728.0)
      - [**Risk:** A rapid, top-down launch could dilute the culture and lead to high volunteer turnover.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=1935.0)
  - [**Local Leadership:** Empower local leaders (e.g., Joel, an NYC influencer) who have deep community connections and can organically build the local hub.](https://fathom.video/calls/753416238?tab=summary&timestamp=1431.0)
