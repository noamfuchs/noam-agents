## Topics

### BRRRR (Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat)

  - [**Goal:** Build long-term wealth through equity and cash flow, not immediate cash-out.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=1173.0)
  - [**Acquisition:** Buy significantly below market value.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=322.0)
      - [**Target:** 65–70% of After Repair Value (ARV).](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=362.0)
      - [**Rationale:** This margin covers all costs (rehab, holding, financing) and ensures a successful refinance.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=379.0)
  - [**Rehab:** Prioritize systems over cosmetics.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=503.0)
      - [**Systems First:** Roof, HVAC, electrical, plumbing. These are expensive to fix and legally required for tenants.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=503.0)
      - [**Cosmetics Second:** Kitchen, baths, flooring, paint. These drive appraisal value but can be deferred.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=503.0)
  - [**Refinance:** Use a DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) loan, which qualifies based on the property's rental income, not your personal income.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=649.0)
      - [**LTV:** 65–70% for non-US citizens; 75–80% for US citizens.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=413.0)
      - [**Cost:** \~$6,000–$8,000 in fees.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=920.0)
      - [**Appraisal:** Use investor-friendly appraisers to maximize ARV.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=812.0)
  - [**Holding Costs:** Budget for all expenses during rehab, including taxes, insurance, and utilities.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=1620.0)

### Flips (Fix & Sell)

  - [**Goal:** Generate large, short-term, taxable profits.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=2225.0)
  - [**Acquisition:** Target owner-occupied neighborhoods with high curb appeal, not just rental areas.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=2409.0)
  - [**Rehab:** Focus on perfection and layout.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=2499.0)
      - [**Layout:** Must be functional for families (e.g., master bath, open concept).](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=2520.0)
      - [**Finishes:** New kitchen, silver appliances, tile baths, new toilets.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=3603.0)
      - [**Key Upgrades:**](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4103.0)
          - [**Windows:** Replace old wood windows with vinyl. This is critical for FHA/VA loan approval and eliminates a major buyer objection.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4143.0)
          - [**Curb Appeal:** Invest in landscaping and exterior paint.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4103.0)
  - [**Financing & Buyers:**](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=3309.0)
      - [**FHA/VA Loans:** High-risk for sellers due to strict inspections and a 90-day holding period requirement.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=3372.0)
      - [**Conventional Loans:** Preferred due to less stringent inspections.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=3529.0)
  - [**Sales & Marketing:**](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4308.0)
      - [**Staging:** Use virtual ($15–$35/photo) or physical ($1k–$1.8k) staging to help buyers visualize the home.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4308.0)
      - [**Photography:** Use professional photos with good lighting (e.g., can lights).](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4525.0)

### Hold-Tail (Buy, Clean, Sell)

  - [**Goal:** Quick profit on properties with a large "as-is" value gap.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4683.0)
  - [**Process:** Buy at a deep discount, perform minimal work (cleaning, minor repairs), and list on the MLS.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4683.0)
  - [**Risk:** Requires expert underwriting to find properties where the "as-is" value is significantly higher than the purchase price.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4627.0)

### Joint Ventures (JVs)

  - [**Goal:** Profit from deals without using your own capital.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4878.0)
  - [**Structure Options:**](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4913.0)
    1.  [**50/50 Partnership:** One partner finds the deal, the other provides all capital and management.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4928.0)
    2.  [**Assignment Fee:** Find a deal and assign the contract to a partner for a flat fee or profit split.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=4976.0)
  - [**Legal:** Some title companies refuse JVs. Find an investor-friendly attorney who will facilitate the assignment.](https://fathom.video/calls/710474938?tab=summary&timestamp=5040.0)
