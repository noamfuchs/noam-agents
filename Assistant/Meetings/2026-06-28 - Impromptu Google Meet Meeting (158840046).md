## Meeting Purpose

[To explore a new data marketplace model for the medical AI industry.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=2429.0)

## Key Takeaways

  - [**The "Cable" Model:** A subscription-based data marketplace ("cable") was proposed to replace the current "warehouse" model (e.g., Protege). This would provide a continuous, real-time data stream, enabling live model retraining and faster development for new modalities.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=941.0)
  - [**Critical Unknowns:** The business model's viability hinges on two unknowns: 1) the difficulty of securing data-sharing agreements with hospitals, and 2) the true market demand for a continuous data stream versus one-off historical datasets.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=2027.0)
  - [**Niche Strategy:** The initial focus should be on a specific niche, such as new pathology stains (e.g., IHC), where historical data is scarce and a continuous stream is essential for training models on emerging techniques.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=3971.0)
  - [**Next Steps:** The team will validate these unknowns by interviewing medical AI startups and data acquisition managers to assess demand and the bureaucratic friction involved.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=2027.0)

## Topics

### Problem: Current Data Marketplaces Are Inefficient

  - [Marketplaces like Protege operate on a "warehouse" model: they acquire large, static data batches and sell them as one-off datasets.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=941.0)
  - [**Limitations of the warehouse model:**](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=60.0)
      - [**Inaccessibility:** The model is enterprise-focused and lacks simple B2C/research access.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=60.0)
      - [**Stale Data:** Data is updated infrequently (e.g., monthly), making it unsuitable for training models on new, rapidly evolving medical techniques.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=360.0)
      - [**High Overhead:** The model requires significant investment in data storage and management.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=1020.0)

### Solution: A Real-Time "Cable" Marketplace

  - [The proposed "cable" model is a subscription service that provides a continuous, real-time data stream directly from hospitals to consumers.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=941.0)
  - [**Key Features:**](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=941.0)
      - [**Automated Pipeline:** A secure pipeline connects directly to hospital systems, pulling anonymized data daily.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=596.0)
      - [**Standardization:** The platform handles data cleaning, standardization, and anonymization.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=1096.0)
      - [**Verification:** The platform verifies the legitimacy of both data providers and consumers.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=941.0)
  - [**Business Model:** The platform would act as a middleman, charging a commission on data transactions.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=1373.0)

### Validation & Strategic Considerations

  - [**Market Demand:**](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=2640.0)
      - [**Hypothesis:** Medical AI startups need a constant data stream for live model retraining and faster development, especially for new modalities.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=4046.0)
      - [**Counterpoint:** For initial product development, a large, static historical dataset might be more efficient than a slow, continuous drip.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=3600.0)
  - [**Provider Friction:**](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=3064.0)
      - [**Challenge:** Hospitals face significant bureaucratic and regulatory hurdles (e.g., Helsinki Committee approval, strict security) to share data.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=3064.0)
      - [**Opportunity:** A plug-and-play "cable" could simplify this process for hospitals, turning their data into a passive revenue stream.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=1020.0)
  - [**Niche Strategy:**](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=3293.0)
      - [**Recommendation:** Focus on a specific niche to avoid a "scattered" approach.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=3293.0)
      - [**Example Niche:** New pathology stains (e.g., IHC).](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=3971.0)
          - [**Why:** Historical data is scarce, making a continuous stream of new samples essential for model training and retraining.](https://fathom.video/share/a2fNoUCyS4qbwAoWAfXC3NXeJY3zZ3Qc?tab=summary&timestamp=4046.0)
