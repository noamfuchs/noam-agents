## Topics

### Group Dynamics & Guest Policy

  - [**Problem:** Guests are monopolizing CEO attention, making core members feel ignored and disengaged.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=95.0)
  - [**Solution:** A new policy will be implemented to protect the core group experience.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=95.0)
      - [**Guest Limit:** 2–4 guests max per session.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=734.0)
      - [**Role:** Guests must observe and not dominate the conversation.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=142.0)
      - [**Logistics:** Guests must register via a Manda link and are not provided coffee.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=790.0)
  - [**Rationale:** The goal is to maintain group intimacy and ensure the CEO's focus remains on the core members.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=95.0)

### Group-Only Meeting Format

  - [**Problem:** Unstructured group-only meetings are ineffective, with members monopolizing time and others disengaging.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=1065.0)
  - [**Solution:** A mandatory, structured format will be created for all group-only meetings.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=1143.0)
      - [**Format:** Include timed activities (e.g., 1-min intros, speed dating) to ensure equitable participation.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=1143.0)
      - [**Rationale:** Provides a ready-to-use plan, eliminating the need for last-minute prep and ensuring a productive session.](https://fathom.video/share/EMusRqAPyZ1U6LJgw54vJy-nrWYu5xUT?tab=summary&timestamp=1143.0)
