# RFS Global

**Goal:** Launch Run For Startups' first English-speaking ("Global"/olim) cohort, expanding beyond the Hebrew-speaking groups. Cornerstone for future global expansion (e.g. New York).
**Co-organizers:** [[dafne-bennatan]] and [[arad-fruchter]] (with [[adam-teer-run-for-startups]] on payments/exceptions, [[lev-wolf]] on cross-group program management).
**Format:** 10-week program, Wednesdays 6:30 (early AM run at Hof Migdalor → coffee + founder/CEO Q&A at FIT house). English only.
**Launch:** 2026-06-10. First speaker: [[sarel-eldor]] (founder of Key Capital, ex-Goldman Sachs Israel CEO).
**Fee:** 500 NIS for community events + admin. Attendance mandatory (3 absences = removal).
**Recruitment:** target was 17; group closed at ~15–16 runners (~50% Israelis with strong English, ~50% olim). Filled largely via personal referrals — marketing to the olim community was hard without an existing community hype.
