# Biliyo

**Goal:** An AI agent that connects to a user's email and (1) finds and cancels/downgrades unwanted subscriptions, (2) analyzes expenses and files insurance/refund claims on the user's behalf — removing the friction that makes consumers leave money on the table.
**Current state:** Pitch stage. Dana pitched it to Noam (2026-06-19) and is recruiting him as **technical co-founder** to build the MVP and own data security, while she runs go-to-market. Noam's assessment: compelling, he can build it — researching feasibility + competition before committing.
**Pitched by / proposed partner:** **Dana** (go-to-market, customer acquisition, business strategy) — see Dana identity note below.

## The thesis (from 2026-06-19 pitch)
- **Problem:** Consumers lose money on forgotten subscriptions and unclaimed insurance refunds because of high friction (complex forms, long calls). Dana found a forgotten $10/mo subscription via an email scan and is personally owed ~₪4,000 in unclaimed refunds (₪2k private services, ₪2k psychology) she never pursued because of the hassle.
- **Inverse-of-Fondo angle:** an investor's partner (company **Fondo**) built a business *adding* friction to block cashback claims; Biliyo does the opposite — *removes* friction for the consumer.
- **MVP scope:** scan email to identify refund opportunities first.
- **Future vision:** proactive services, e.g. auto-set a cancellation reminder for a student discount when a degree ends.

## Monetization
- **Free tier:** subscription management (cancel / downgrade).
- **Paid tier:** insurance-refund filing at a **~5% success fee** on recovered amounts.
- **Open technical challenge:** how to collect the success fee when the refund is paid directly to the user. Candidate: monitor the user's email for refund confirmation, then charge a pre-authorized card.
